Aditya Birla High School (+2) was established in 1948. It is affiliated to Jharkhand Academic Council, Ranchi. It is co-educational and the medium of instruction is Hindi. The school is from Class I to Class XII. The school is located in a serene and quiet environment of the village Tirla in Singhpur in Chota Muri in Ranchi district at Jharkhand. It is nearly 65km away from Ranchi towards Purulia in West Bengal. The school is the hallmark of this area for its excellent facilities and amenities.
